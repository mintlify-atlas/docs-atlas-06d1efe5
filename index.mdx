## What is MiroFish?

MiroFish is a multi-agent swarm intelligence engine that lets you predict future outcomes by creating and simulating high-fidelity digital worlds. By extracting seed information from the real world—such as breaking news, policy drafts, financial signals, or even novel stories—MiroFish automatically constructs parallel digital environments where thousands of AI agents with independent personalities, long-term memory, and behavioral logic interact and evolve.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Seed Data">
    Provide source materials like reports, news articles, or story content that define your scenario
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Knowledge Graph">
    MiroFish extracts entities, relationships, and memories to construct a GraphRAG representation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Agents">
    Automatically generate agent profiles with unique personalities and behavioral logic
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Simulation">
    Launch parallel simulations where agents interact on social platforms and evolve over time
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Insights">
    Create detailed prediction reports and interact with the ReportAgent to explore findings
  </Step>
</Steps>
